Granada + Seville - May 21-26, 2026

From Barcelona to Andalusia and back, with two days in Granada and three in Seville.

This itinerary uses the flight, train, and return flight you provided: VY2012 to Granada on May 21, a 10:30 AM train to Seville on May 23 arriving at 12 PM, and VY2219 back to Barcelona on May 26.

Trip Snapshot

  • Start: Barcelona to Granada on VY2012, Thursday, May 21, 2026
  • Mid-leg transfer: Granada to Seville train, Saturday, May 23, 2026 at 10:30 AM
  • Return: Seville to Barcelona on VY2219, Tuesday, May 26, 2026
  • Style: Moorish heritage, old quarters, viewpoints, tapas, and walkable evenings

Overall Route Logic

The Granada portion centers on the Alhambra, the historic core, and hilltop sunset views. The Seville portion pivots to the royal palace, cathedral district, Santa Cruz, riverfront, and Triana.

Transport Strategy

Use short taxi or airport-bus transfers on flight days, keep Granada mostly walk-and-cab friendly, then rely on central walking routes and occasional taxis in Seville before the return flight.

Best Use Pattern

Keep the heaviest monument visits early in the day, leave the Albaicin and Triana stretches for the cooler evening hours, and treat the train day as a light-transition day rather than packing a full museum schedule into it.
