Trip Snapshot

  • Base: Catalonia Magdalenes Hotel
  • Style: Culture, Gaudi, scenic walking, day trip

Overall Route Logic

The itinerary groups nearby neighborhoods and transit corridors to keep each day focused: medieval core first, museum-heavy Montjuic next, Gaudi landmarks after that, then a dedicated Montserrat day trip, and a light final day through the market and waterfront before departure.

Transport Strategy

The document recommends the Hola Barcelona Travel Card for unlimited metro, bus, tram, and urban rail access, including the airport metro connection.

Sunday, May 17, 2026

The Medieval Soul

Compact walking day

A tightly grouped route through the Gothic Quarter, El Born, and Sant Pere, covering major culture stops in roughly a one-mile inner-city arc.

Morning

Walk through the Gothic Quarter to Barcelona Cathedral. The source notes Sunday worship hours in the morning and tourist visits later in the day.

Route note: about 5 minutes via Carrer del Bisbe.

Open map route

Afternoon

Cross into El Born for artisan shops and the Picasso Museum Barcelona.

Route note: around 11 minutes on foot via Carrer de l'Argenteria.

Cathedral to Picasso Museum

Evening

Continue to the Palau de la Musica Catalana for a concert-night finish.

Route note: around 9 minutes on foot via Carrer d'En Giralt el Pellicer.

Picasso Museum to Palau

Monday, May 18, 2026

International Museum Day

Museums + boulevard walk

A museum-heavy morning on Montjuic followed by a return to the center for Gaudi facades on Passeig de Gracia.

Morning

Head to Montjuic for Fundacio Joan Miro and MNAC, both highlighted in the source for free admission that day.

Transit note: bus 55 from Placa de Catalunya or L3 to Paral·lel plus the funicular.

Placa de Catalunya to Joan Miro

Between Stops

Walk between Fundacio Joan Miro and MNAC.

Route note: roughly 9 minutes via Passeig de Santa Madrona.

Joan Miro to MNAC

Afternoon

Return to Passeig de Gracia to admire Casa Batllo and Casa Mila from the boulevard.

Transit note: from Poble Sec, take L3 to Passeig de Gracia; facade-to-facade stroll is about 8 minutes.

MNAC to Casa Batllo Casa Batllo to Casa Mila

Tuesday, May 19, 2026

The Gaudi Legacy

Hotel to hilltop to basilica

A route built around booked Gaudi highlights, using one direct bus uphill and one efficient crosstown bus back toward the Eixample.

Morning

Walk from the hotel to Placa de Catalunya, then take Bus 24 straight to Park Guell.

Route note: about 6 minutes walking to the stop, then roughly 32 minutes on the bus.

Hotel to Placa de Catalunya Placa de Catalunya to Park Guell

Midday

Explore Park Guell, with the source strongly recommending advance booking for the Monumental Zone.

Visit note: source lists opening hours as 8:00 AM to 9:30 PM in May.

Open Park Guell on map

Afternoon

Take the V19 toward Barceloneta, get off at Pg de Sant Joan - Rossello, and walk to Sagrada Familia.

Route note: around 27 minutes total plus a final 10-minute walk.

Park Guell to Sagrada Familia

Wednesday, May 20, 2026

Montserrat Day Trip

Regional rail excursion

A full mountain day using the R5 line from Placa d'Espanya, followed by either the cable car or rack railway ascent to the abbey.

Morning Departure

Start at Placa d'Espanya and board the R5 train toward Manresa-Baixador.

Transit note: the document describes roughly hourly departures and about one hour to the mountain base.

Hotel to Placa d'Espanya Placa d'Espanya to Monistrol

Choose Your Ascent

Either get off at Montserrat-Aeri for the cable car or continue to Monistrol de Montserrat for the Cremallera rack railway.

Route note: total time to the abbey is about 1 hour 36 minutes via the rack railway.

Cable car option to abbey Rack railway option to abbey

On the Mountain

See the Black Madonna and take the Sant Joan Funicular for higher views if conditions and schedule align.

Visit note: the source recommends timed booking for the Madonna due to queues.

Abbey to Sant Joan Funicular

Thursday, May 21, 2026

Local Flavors and Departure

Short city finale

A low-friction final route from the market to the harbor and possibly the beach, ending with either a quick taxi or a longer public-transit airport connection.

Morning

Walk from the hotel to Mercat de la Boqueria for breakfast, ideally early to beat the crowds.

Route note: about 10 minutes on foot via Carrer de la Portaferrissa.

Hotel to La Boqueria

Final Stroll

Continue down La Rambla to Port Vell, then extend to Barceloneta Beach if there is enough time before departure.

Route note: around 17 minutes to Port Vell, plus about 20 more to Barceloneta Beach.
